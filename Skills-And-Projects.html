Video Editing

As my skills grew and I got better at editing, I decided to switch to Adobe's Premiere Pro, a more advanced editing program with more functionality. The switch came with more than just the functionality, the program looked better visually, was easy to navigate, and worked smoothly with little to no crashes. This is similar to software development where you find better ways to do things that help explore your creativity and innovate in the field.
